Filipino Adobo

Adobo is the unofficial national dish of the Philippines, known for its perfect balance of salty, sour, and savory flavors. This beloved comfort food features tender meat braised in a rich sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs and drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and brown the chicken pieces on all sides.
  3. Add onions and cook until softened, then pour in the marinade.
  4. Bring to a boil, then simmer covered for 45 minutes until tender.
  5. Remove lid and cook on medium-high heat until sauce thickens.
  6. Serve hot over steamed white rice.
